# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 20, 2026

This is a personal project ("OpenClaw") used by Pascual Zamudio for personal productivity.

## What data we collect
- This tool runs locally on your machine.
- When using Google integration (Gmail, Calendar), it only accesses data you explicitly authorize.
- No data is collected or stored by this project on external servers except what you choose to sync via Google services.

## Google Services
When you connect your Google account, OpenClaw uses the official Google APIs to read Calendar and Gmail **only on your device**. No data is sent to third parties.
